One Two Red Blue


In my Intro to Robotics, we used a robot to make a light painting photograph. We used a modified industrial Puma 260 robot. There were several steps to this project. First was to find the DH parameters of the RRR robot. Next we had to find the inverse kinematics for the Puma. After testing our IK solutions, our team created a set of points for the Puma to “paint”. We chose a scene from Space Invaders. The result of the long exposure photo is below.
