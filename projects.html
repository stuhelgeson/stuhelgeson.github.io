Wee Wee Pad Dispenser

Given the details of the decive in the patent, I had to design and build a working prototype to demonstrate the fesability of an automated house breaking pad dispensor. The project started with brainstorming ideas on a piece of paper, and grew into the fabrication of a functioning prototype. Along the way I learned a ton, from figuring out what a microcontroller is to building molds and working with composites. It was by no means easy but a challenge I would accept over and over.
