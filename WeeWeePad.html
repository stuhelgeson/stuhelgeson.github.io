Wee Wee Pad Dispenser


Given the details of the device in the patent, I had to design and build a working prototype to demonstrate the feasibility of an automated house breaking pad dispenser. The project started with brainstorming ideas on a piece of paper, and grew into the fabrication of a functioning prototype. Along the way, I learned a ton, from figuring out what a microcontroller is to building molds and working with composites. It was by no means easy but a challenge I would accept again and again.
